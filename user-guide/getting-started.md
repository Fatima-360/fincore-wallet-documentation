## 3. Frequently Asked Questions
**Q: Why is the VAT rate different for different customers?**
A: Under the **EU VAT Directive**, digital services are taxed at the rate of the *customer's* country, not yours.

**Q: Can I edit an invoice after it is generated?**
A: No. To maintain audit-ready records, invoices are locked once issued. If you need to make a change, you must issue a **Credit Note**.
